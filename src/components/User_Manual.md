# Mbale Innovators Hub - User Manual

## 1. Introduction

This manual provides instructions on how to use the Mbale Innovators Hub application. It covers the functionalities available to Students, Supervisors, and Administrators.

## 2. For All Users

### Registration
1. Navigate to the "Register" page.
2. Fill in your name, email, password, faculty, and department.
3. Click "Register". You will be redirected to the login page.

### Login
1. Navigate to the "Login" page.
2. Enter your registered email and password.
3. Click "Login". You will be redirected to the homepage.

## 3. For Students

### Submitting a Project
1. After logging in, navigate to your "Dashboard".
2. Fill out the "Submit a New Project" form with all required details, including title, description, technologies, and an optional PDF document.
3. Click "Submit Project". Your project will be sent for supervisor review.

### Viewing Your Projects
- On your dashboard, you can see a list of all projects you have submitted and their current status (pending, approved, rejected).

## 4. For Supervisors

### Reviewing Projects
1. After logging in, navigate to your "Dashboard".
2. You will see a list of "Projects Awaiting Review".
3. Click "View Details" to see the full project information.
4. On the detail page, you can click "Approve" or "Reject". If rejecting, you will be prompted to provide a reason.

## 5. For Administrators

### Viewing Analytics
- On your dashboard, you will see an overview of platform statistics, including total projects and charts for status and category distribution.

### Managing Users
- Below the analytics, you will find a table of all registered users.
- You can change a user's role by clicking "Edit Role", selecting a new role, and clicking "Save".
- You can delete a user by clicking the "Delete" button.
